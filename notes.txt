 position:static|relative|absolute|fixed|sticky

 Relative position:-a part of normal document flow

 -re-positioning of an element from original position to new position
 top,left,bottom and right
  
  absolute position:-
  remove the normal document flow and reposition the element
  based on top, right, bottom and left offset values
  it will take the other element space which are in flow 

  fixed position:-
  it is with respect to the browser
  the content not moved
